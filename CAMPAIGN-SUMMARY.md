## The Story

Justin Kasieta's campaign is built on one of the most compelling personal stories in American politics:

### Background
- **Born and raised:** Upper Peninsula of Michigan
- **Age 13:** Father diagnosed with aggressive cancer
- **Age 13-15:** Lost his father; became caretaker for four younger siblings
- **Foster care:** Entered Michigan's system, lived in 4 homes, attended 4 high schools
- **The betrayal:** State of Michigan took $18,000+ in his Social Security survivor benefits to reimburse foster care costs—without telling him

### The Advocate
- **Discovered the truth:** Found out years later that the state had seized his benefits
- **Went public:** Testified before Michigan Legislature supporting Senate Bill 18
- **National voice:** Featured on NPR, CBS News, MSN, Georgia Public Broadcasting
- **Real impact:** 
  - 15 states reformed their laws
  - Trump administration issued warnings to states
  - Brought attention to 25,000+ foster children affected nationally ($174M/year seized)
- **Respected authority:** Amy Harfeld of Children's Advocacy Institute at University of San Diego called the issue critical

### Education & Career
- **University of Michigan graduate** (on scholarships)
- **Government experience:** Interned in Michigan state legislature and U.S. Congress
- **Professional background:** Finance and government
- **Current role:** National advocate for foster care reform

## Platform (7 Priorities)

### 1. **Protecting Michigan's Children** 🏆 *Signature Issue*
- Federal ban on states seizing foster children's Social Security survivor benefits
- Full transparency and accountability in child welfare
- Every child deserves the support their parents paid for

### 2. **Family Values & Foster Care Reform**
- Strengthen families to prevent kids from entering foster care
- Support foster parents who open their homes
- Hold child welfare agencies accountable
- Make success stories the rule, not the exception

### 3. **Economic Opportunity**
- Bring manufacturing jobs back to Michigan
- Support small businesses
- Reduce regulations that hurt Main Street
- Create opportunity for those starting from nothing

### 4. **Education**
- College access and scholarships for students who need them
- Expand vocational training for skilled trades
- School choice for parents
- Make education the path forward

### 5. **Government Accountability**
- Shrink wasteful government spending
- End bureaucracy that hurts instead of helps
- Transparency in how taxpayer dollars are spent
- Make government work for the people

### 6. **Veterans & Military Families**
- Protect all military family benefits, including survivor benefits for children
- Honor those who served
- Support veterans with the care and respect they've earned

### 7. **Rural Michigan & the Upper Peninsula**
- Invest in broadband and infrastructure
- Support natural resource industries
- Represent rural communities that are often forgotten
