# Justin Kasieta for U.S. Senate - Michigan 2026

**"From Foster Care to the Fight for Michigan"**

Professional campaign website for Justin Kasieta's 2026 U.S. Senate campaign.

## About Justin

Justin Kasieta grew up on Michigan's Upper Peninsula. At 13, after losing his father to cancer, he became the caretaker for his four younger siblings. He entered foster care, moved through 4 homes and 4 high schools, and discovered that the State of Michigan had seized over $18,000 of his Social Security survivor benefits to reimburse foster care costs—without telling him.

He challenged the system, became a national advocate for foster children's rights, testified before the Michigan Legislature, and was featured on NPR, CBS News, and other major outlets. His advocacy helped reform policies in 15 states.

Justin graduated from the University of Michigan on scholarships and now runs for U.S. Senate to protect Michigan's children and families.
